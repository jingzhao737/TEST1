About the Artist

I’m a visual artist working at the intersection of traditional craft and digital experimentation—where paint meets pixels.

My work spans oil painting, digital art, sculpture, and installation. I don’t believe in medium boundaries—the idea dictates the tools, not the other way around. Every piece is a conversation between intention and accident.

Based in Shanghai, exhibiting internationally. My work has appeared in galleries across Asia and Europe, and in private collections worldwide.
